About Us

Our restaurant has a rich and fascinating history that is deeply intertwined with the local community. It all began nearly a century ago, when the building that now houses our restaurant was originally constructed. Over the years, the building has been home to a variety of businesses, from a hardware store to a printing press. But it wasn't until the 1970s that it became a restaurant.

The restaurant was founded by a local couple who wanted to bring something new and exciting to the dining scene in our city. They drew inspiration from their travels around the world, creating a menu that featured flavors and ingredients from a variety of cultures. The restaurant quickly became a hit with locals and visitors alike, known for its delicious food and warm, inviting atmosphere.
